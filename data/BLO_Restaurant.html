I can not wait to return to !
Jun 2024 • family
Last Saturday, for my birthday, my son said to me "I invite you to the best meat restaurant in Lyon". I've made a few before but this one really beats it. First, a perfect welcome; as we wanted a prime rib, we were taken care of and the differences between the 4 on the menu were clearly explained to us. As I had already tasted the other 3, we took a Gallician beef; there were 4 of us and the ribs are for 2 or 3 but the waiter (owner?) told us that he had one of 1.4kg which could serve 4 if we took starters. We had a plate of bellota and a burrata for 4, both were excellent. In the meantime, the sommelier wisely recommended a Cahors which perfectly matched the Gallice, actually stronger in taste than a Simmental or a Wagyu. The desserts are presented on a slate, a very original process which allows you to get an idea of what you are going to eat and the ones we chose were delicious, accompanied by a quite stunning Coteau d'Aix in the glass. In short, everything was perfect; given the price, it's not the canteen for every day (alas!) but I plan to return there soon enough...for the Wagyu of course so I say "See you soon"

Disappointing
Aug 2020 • none
I was hoping for a lot more from B.L.O. given the reviews, but unfortunately we left quite disappointed. We felt the staff were very inattentive (we sat without menus for almost 15 minutes, our finished plates sat in front of us for ages, bill took a while etc). But most importantly the food just wasn't great. We had the Italian sausage for the starter which turned out to be a kind of salami (I don't know why but I was expecting a cooked / hot sausage kind of gaucho style). Also, they took the sausage / salami from the wall where other guests were sitting. I know it is common in some places to hang the salami in front of guests but it was a bit off putting to see it first as a decoration and then served to us - couldn't the kitchen keep some on hand, separate to the decoration purpose ones? Also it was cut so thick that half of it was completely inedible (even uncuttable), way too hard like a rock. We each ordered a fillet steak and the meat was good, though just semi warm for whatever reason (this was the best part of the meal!). The sides were not nice - the fries were greasy and the vegetables were kind of sloppy. We were going to order dessert as the mousse looked good but it took so long to get any attention that we just decided to get the bill instead. It is an extremely rare occasion when I don't leave a tip but unfortunately this was one of those occasions, a decision based on the experience as a whole. Total cost: around €100.
