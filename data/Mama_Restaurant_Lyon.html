About
Mama Restaurant Lyon is a lively place where a diverse group of people come together to enjoy deliciously traditional French dishes, that honor the capital of gastronomy. After dinner, swing by the bar that comes alive to the sounds of the «live» stage, in a true Lyon fashion, a city that is known to be welcoming and joyful. Mama welcomes everyone!
