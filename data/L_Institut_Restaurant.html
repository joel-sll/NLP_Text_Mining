Gastronomique delight in the centre of Lyon
Sep 2023 ‚Ä¢ couples
A real treat to visit such as this institute geared to the teaching of gastronomical excellence in terms of the food, service, setting and ambience. It was not perfect but then it's teaching the future army of chefs, waiters and waitresses some of whom have not been very long at this centre of gastronomique excellemce. But some aspects of it reached great heights, others to be improved upon. The quality of the ingredients, as one would expect, were top notch and their use in each plate excellent. My wife had a wonderful monkfish main dish and a salmon starter which her first taste elicited the comment of "divine". I would use the same word for the first fork of the "Pate en Croute" which had Pork, Duck and Veal encased in a delicious pastry crust with a few different types of garnish, sauce etc. Excellent dish. The only real complaint I had was my main course of a sous vide beef cooked until it literally melted in your mouth was, surprisingly, quite salty. It was served with a piece of Fois Gras on top with a rich red wine jus. My first bite was a bit of everything so I then tried everything individually to establish that the jus was mostly the culprit but the final presentation had some salt crystals over the fois gras which were unnecessary and added unfortunately to increase the saltiness. Service was both timely, competent, and friendly. The suggested glasses of wine for each course were excellent and matched well. A second minor criticism was the presentation could be improved, especially the Pate en Croute. It was a 5 star dish with 3.5 star plating! (I'm being critical I know but I think constructive criticism is vital for improvements necessary given many of these young folk will go onto to work or, hopefully, even own Michelin star restaurants). We finished with a desert, beautifully plated and a cheese selection cut from a number from a laden trolley by our assigned waitress. Cheeses were perfect. The building itself is stunning and on the corner of a square which adds to the overall impression of a special locale. Overall then, a great lunch which will long be remembered. Thank you from my wife and I for giving us such a unique and wonderful experience. We hope to return!!
